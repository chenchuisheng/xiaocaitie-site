XiaoCaiTie Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-04-29

XiaoCaiTie (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when using the XiaoCaiTie app and e-ink smart fridge magnet device.

1. Core Principle

We are designed with a strong privacy-first approach. Wherever possible, your travel data, badges, and memories are processed locally on your device. We do not operate systems for tracking or selling user data.

2. Information We May Use

  • Location data (to unlock travel badges)
  • Photo library access (only when you choose to generate badges)
  • Device information (for compatibility and performance)
  • Login information (Apple / WeChat login if used)

3. How We Use Data

We use data only to enable core features:

  • Unlocking travel location badges
  • Generating personalized travel memories
  • Displaying badges on e-ink device
  • Improving app performance

4. Local Processing

Most data processing happens locally on your device. We do not maintain a system that collects or monitors your full travel history.

5. Photo & Location Policy

If you grant permission:

  • Photos may be used to generate travel badges
  • Location may be used to identify visited places

We do not upload or store your full photo library or continuous location tracking data.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade user data. We only share data when required by law or when you explicitly use third-party services.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Apple Sign-In
  • WeChat Login
  • Cloud sync services (optional)

8. Your Rights

  • Disable permissions anytime
  • Delete app data locally
  • Stop using cloud sync
